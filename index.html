FREELANCE

Interactive installation at Rockheim - SMILE Music video

A computer generated wireframe figure in pure 80s esthetic appears and mimics the movement of anybody who steps in front of the installation. The figure is modeled after the wireframe figure that makes an apperance in a music video for the 1983 song "Smile" by Will Powers. The installation is a part of a temporary exhibition dedicated to Lynn Goldsmith and the exhibition is called "SMILE - Lynn Goldsmith in the 80s"
